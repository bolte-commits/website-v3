    Know your body, with clarity.

    Our AI-powered scans go beyond collecting endless numbers they highlight what truly matters for your health. Instead of overwhelming you with metrics, Body Insight distills them into insights you can act on. Because when it comes to your body, more isnt always better better is better.

    Reports delivered instantly on our app

    The Body Insight app delivers your results the moment your test is complete no waiting, no delays. From a single dashboard, you can explore every detail: body composition, visceral fat, bone health, metabolism, and VOmax performance. Track changes over time, compare past scans, and keep all your health data secure and accessible anytime.

    Features to empower your goals.

    • Wake up to your sleep score.

      Sleep quality is influenced by factors such as sleep duration, bedtime consistency, how often you wake up, and time spent in each sleep stage. Sleep score analyzes these factors every night and provides a classification and a score. Youll see how the score is calculated, so you can understand the quality of your sleep and learn how to make it more restorative.

    • Keep an eye on sleep apnea.

      Sleep apnea is a condition where people experience repeated interruptions in their regular respiratory pattern during sleep. If untreated, it can lead to increased risk of hypertension, type 2 diabetes, cardiac issues, and more. Series 11 can monitor for breathing disturbances over time and notify you of possible sleep apnea.11

    • The Vitals app. Your daily health status, stat.

      Quickly see your overnight health data including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration and get notified if multiple metrics are ever outside your typical range.6

    • Follow your heart on the ECG app.

      With Series 11 you can check for signs of atrial fibrillation, or AFib, right from your wrist by using the ECG app to generate a single-lead electrocardiogram.12

    • Stay on the pulse with the Heart Rate app.

      You can check your heart rate at any time and get automatic notifications for a high or low heart rate or an irregular rhythm.13

    • Cycle Tracking keeps you in the loop.

      Since cycles vary, the best way to understand whats normal for you is by tracking your menstrual cycle. You can use Cycle Tracking to log your period, record symptoms, and get predictions about your next period or fertile window.14 It can also notify you if a cycle deviation is detected.

    • Get retrospective ovulation estimates.

      Series 11 has advanced sensors that track your wrist temperature while you sleep. The Cycle Tracking app uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning.

    • Measure your blood oxygen. Its a breathtaking innovation.

      The Health app on iPhone uses data from the sensor in Apple Watch to enable you to take on-demand readings of your blood oxygen as well as background readings, day and night.15

    Accuracy

    Our approach.

    Every test at Body Insight uses medical-grade technology the same systems trusted by hospitals, researchers, and elite sports teams. DEXA imaging pinpoints fat, muscle, and bone with unmatched precision. RMR and VOmax testing rely on lab-standard metabolic carts and breath-by-breath analysis, giving you results far beyond consumer wearables or smart scales.

    Why it matters.

    Accurate data means better decisions. From tailoring nutrition to preventing injuries and spotting long-term risks, precision insights help you and your coach or doctor make changes that actually work. With Body Insight, youre not guessing youre measuring.

    Accessibility

    Our approach.

    We designed Body Insight to be within everyones reach. Our mobile health vans bring advanced testing directly to your neighborhood, while our expanding city hubs make it easier than ever to book an appointment.

    Why it matters.

    Healthcare data should not be limited to labs and hospitals. By putting world-class diagnostics on wheels, we remove barriers of distance and convenience so you can take control of your health without changing your routine.

    Affordability

    Our approach.

    We believe gold-standard testing should not come with gold-plated prices. By operating dedicated vans and serving large communities, we deliver clinical-grade scans and metabolic tests at a fraction of hospital costs.

    Why it matters.

    When precision is affordable, more people can track their health, catch risks early, and improve performance. With Body Insight, science is no longer a privilege its accessible healthcare for all.
